What We Do for You?

MERCYDA'Z INTERNATIONAL PVT. LTD® is a branch of Mercyda'z Instrumentation Inc. (formerly Digila Devices India Pvt. Ltd.), located in Sulthan Bathery, North Kerala, India, established in March 1994. We are an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 24510, IATF 16949, ISO 14001 and UN Regulation ECR 10 approved and certified company, engaged in the manufacturing of a wide range of communication, industrial, automotive, electronic, and electrical products. We are a leading manufacturer of AIS-018 certified Electronic Vehicle Speed Governors and AIS-140 certified Vehicle Tracking Systems under the trusted brand MERCYDA. Our product portfolio also includes advanced vehicle surveillance and safety solutions such as Dashcams, 4G Dome Cameras, 4G WiFi Cameras, and CCTV DVR Monitors, delivering complete visibility, security, and operational efficiency. Our company is approved and certified by ARAI (Automotive Research Association of India), ICAT (International Centre for Automotive Technology), SAMEER Chennai (Society for Applied Microwave Electronics Engineering and Research), the Weights and Measures Department, Government of India, the Department of Telecommunications, Government of India, and the Ministry of Spain and GSO.
