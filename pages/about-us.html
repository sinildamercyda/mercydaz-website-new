About Mercyda'z International Pvt. Ltd.®

Engineering Excellence and Innovation Since 1994

Established in March 1994, Mercyda'z International Pvt. Ltd®. is a leading manufacturer and provider of innovative automotive, electronic, industrial, and communication solutions. Headquartered in Sulthan Bathery, Kerala, India, the company is a subsidiary of Mercyda'z Instrumentation Inc.

Mercyda'z International Pvt. Ltd®. holds multiple industry certifications, including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 24510, IATF 16949, ISO 14001, and UN regulation ECR 10, along with approvals from ARAI, ICAT, SAMEER, and the Department of Telecommunications, Government of India.

With a strong focus on technological advancement and customer-centric solutions, Mercyda'z continues to set new benchmarks across industries.

Our Mission

To maintain our leadership position in technological and engineering domains by delivering cutting-edge technology, tailor-made solutions, quality products, and cost-effective services that drive results and achieve excellence.

Our Vision

To be the preferred technological and engineering solutions and service provider to our esteemed clients, working closely with them to create long-term value and prosperity for all stakeholders.

Our Legacy Begins With Vision

Since 1994, our journey has been driven by innovation, determination, and a commitment to engineering excellence. The early products showcased in this archive represent the foundation of our technological evolution and the passion that continues to guide us today.

What started as pioneering communication and vehicle safety solutions has grown into a trusted legacy of reliability, quality, and forward-thinking design.

Legacy Archive

Foundational products from 1994 that pioneered our engineering excellence. No longer in production, but forever part of our story.

1994
Communication

MERCYDA Coin Pay Phones

Robust public payphone systems with integrated coin validation, call metering, and optional printer support. Designed for heavy-duty environments such as railways and bus stations, featuring battery backup, memory storage, and reliable operation during power interruptions.

  • CDMA / GSM Coin Pay Phone 1500-call memory, battery backup, optional printer
  • CDMA / GSM P.C.O Multi-coin support, programmable billing, LCD display
  • Alpha Numeric / Numeric P.C.O / Numeric P.C.O - 42v DTMF dialing, call timer, tone/pulse compatible
  • Wall Mounted Pay Phone 03D(W.M) Rugged enclosure, external battery support
  • Table Top Pay Phone (03D DLX / 03D ALX) Redial facility, EPABX compatible, compact design
Discontinued
2001
Billing Systems

MERCYDA 2001 STD PCO Monitors

Automated billing and call monitoring units built on low-power microchip technology with keyboard-programmable settings. Provided accurate call duration tracking, tariff calculation, and billing display with optional printing, making them ideal for commercial Public Call Office operations.

  • Citizen 555 Basic call metering, keyboard programmable
  • Epson 460 & Epson 42v LCD billing display, printer interface support
  • PCO-2001 Pro Programmable pulse rate, tariff settings, memory storage
  • PCO-2001 Enterprise Multi-line billing, advanced reporting capability
Discontinued
1994
Monitoring

MERCYDA Local Call Monitor & Conference Unit

Professional call monitoring and conferencing solutions with speaker output, paging functionality, and multi-line connectivity. Compatible with standard telephone exchanges and capable of operating on battery or mains power, supporting office communication control and coordination.

  • LCM-02D 8DIGITS 2-line monitoring, speaker output, paging support
  • LCM-07D 16DIGITS 4-line monitoring, display unit, recording output
  • Conference Facility Device-05D 4-party conference bridge, battery & mains operation
Discontinued
1994
Tele communication

MERCYDA CDMA /GSM Coin Pay Phone

Early mobile communication solutions featuring CDMA and GSM technology for public payphone applications. These devices provided reliable wireless connectivity, integrated coin validation, and call metering, enabling communication in areas without fixed-line infrastructure.

Discontinued
1994
Billing Systems

MERCYDA Electronic Cash Register

An early electronic cash register solution designed for retail environments, featuring a digital display, programmable keys, and basic inventory tracking. This device provided efficient transaction processing and improved accuracy over traditional mechanical cash registers.

Discontinued

From Dream to Reality: The Mercyda’z Story

Mr. Biju Paulose

A Visionary Leader in Technological & Engineering Industry

Mercyda’z International Pvt. Ltd is an ISO9001-2015, ISO 24510, IATF 16949, ISO 14001, and UN regulation ECR 10 certified company. Founded in 1994 in Sulthan Bathery, North Kerala, its vision is to be the preferred technological and engineering solutions provider. Since its establishment, it has catered to dozens of clients, providing top-notch service and support.

The brand name MERCYDA stands for Mercy Dear Amma. Mr. Paulose explains: “Every mother trusts her child, it’s the duty of a child to prove the trust, and as son I am fulfilling my duty. I feel happy and proud when others praise my mother’s name through my brand name.”

Owing to his relentless commitment, Mercyda’z has carved a niche in India’s Tech & Engineering sector. The company has advanced infrastructure across departments like R&D, Production, Quality Control, Logistics, Sales, Marketing, and Designing, all managed by highly experienced professionals. Mr. Paulose highlights: “We have channel partners across India, Middle East and African countries. We provide one-to-one solution for every customer. Our specialties include 24*7 customer service, online technical assistance, customized designs as per requirements and cost-effective services.”

Mercyda’z has been approved and certified by ARAI, SAMEER, ICAT, GSO, and the Ministry of Spain. It is also a certified member of WQA India and recognized under the Trademark Act, 1999. The company manufactures AIS 018 certified Electronic Vehicle Speed Governors, AIS 140 certified Vehicle Tracking Systems under the brand MERCYDA.

Mr. Paulose likens the company's growth to a banyan tree: “The longer it can survive, the deeper the roots can penetrate the soil... The longer the brand can cope with the market’s challenges, the deeper it can penetrate into it.” This commitment to constant growth and adaptation has kept Mercyda’z resilient through market uncertainties.

Customer satisfaction is at the core of Mercyda’z: “Our Customers are our family. Happiness of our clients is what the company aims at achieving. Without the happiness of customers there is no MERCYDA.” The team conducts annual surveys, real-time tracking, and monitoring to ensure every customer need is met with transparency and efficiency.

Over the years, Mercyda’z has prioritized long-term client relationships over short-term monetary goals, creating value and trust. Mr. Paulose emphasizes teamwork: “My success is dedicated to my team’s hard work, dedication and determination. Self-reliance is what our nation needs right now. We depend upon China for most things, we should develop our country. I pledge solemnly for Make in India.”
