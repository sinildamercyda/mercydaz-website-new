About Mercyda'z International Pvt. Ltd.

Engineering Excellence and Innovation Since 1994

Established in March 1994, Mercyda'z International Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer and provider of innovative automotive, electronic, industrial, and communication solutions. Headquartered in Sulthan Bathery, Kerala, India, the company is a subsidiary of Mercyda'z Instrumentation Inc.

Mercyda'z International Pvt. Ltd. holds multiple industry certifications, including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 24510, IATF 16949, ISO 14001, and UN regulation ECR 10, along with approvals from ARAI, ICAT, SAMEER, and the Department of Telecommunications, Government of India.

With a strong focus on technological advancement and customer-centric solutions, Mercyda'z continues to set new benchmarks across industries.

From Dream to Reality: The Mercyda’z Story

Mr. Biju Poulose

A Visionary Leader in Technological & Engineering Industry

Mercyda’z International Pvt. Ltd is an ISO9001-2015, ISO 24510, IATF 16949, ISO 14001, and UN regulation ECR 10 certified company. Founded in 1994 in Sulthan Bathery, North Kerala, its vision is to be the preferred technological and engineering solutions provider. Since its establishment, it has catered to dozens of clients, providing top-notch service and support.

The brand name MERCYDA stands for Mercy Dear Amma. Mr. Poulose explains: “Every mother trusts her child, it’s the duty of a child to prove the trust, and as son I am fulfilling my duty. I feel happy and proud when others praise my mother’s name through my brand name.”

Owing to his relentless commitment, Mercyda’z has carved a niche in India’s Tech & Engineering sector. The company has advanced infrastructure across departments like R&D, Production, Quality Control, Logistics, Sales, Marketing, and Designing, all managed by highly experienced professionals. Mr. Poulose highlights: “We have channel partners across India, Middle East and African countries. We provide one-to-one solution for every customer. Our specialties include 24*7 customer service, online technical assistance, customized designs as per requirements and cost-effective services.”

Mercyda’z has been approved and certified by ARAI, SAMEER, ICAT, GSO, and the Ministry of Spain. It is also a certified member of WQA India and recognized under the Trademark Act, 1999. The company manufactures AIS 018 certified Electronic Vehicle Speed Governors, AIS 140 certified Vehicle Tracking Systems, and RO Water Purifiers under the brand MERCYDA.

Mr. Poulose likens the company's growth to a banyan tree: “The longer it can survive, the deeper the roots can penetrate the soil... The longer the brand can cope with the market’s challenges, the deeper it can penetrate into it.” This commitment to constant growth and adaptation has kept Mercyda’z resilient through market uncertainties.

Customer satisfaction is at the core of Mercyda’z: “Our Customers are our family. Happiness of our clients is what the company aims at achieving. Without the happiness of customers there is no MERCYDA.” The team conducts annual surveys, real-time tracking, and monitoring to ensure every customer need is met with transparency and efficiency.

Over the years, Mercyda’z has prioritized long-term client relationships over short-term monetary goals, creating value and trust. Mr. Poulose emphasizes teamwork: “My success is dedicated to my team’s hard work, dedication and determination. Self-reliance is what our nation needs right now. We depend upon China for most things, we should develop our country. I pledge solemnly for Make in India.”
