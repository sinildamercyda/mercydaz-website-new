ECU Engineering

Custom ECU Engineering & Productization

End-to-end delivery

Key features and compliance of our Mercyda ECU includes ARAI approval, ICAT certification, AIS-140 compliance, PC-TEN approval, IP67 rating, BS-IV compatibility, BCU support, and high tamper resistance. Deliverables include:

  • ARAI certified for AIS and CMVR compliance
  • ICAT tested for durability, performance, and reliability
  • Fully compliant with AIS-140 standards for vehicle tracking and emergency alerts
  • PC-TEN certified for protocol compliance and data integrity
  • IP67 rated dust-tight and water-immersible enclosure
  • BS-IV (BS4) compatible with accurate engine data acquisition
  • BCU integration support for centralized vehicle control
  • Advanced tamper detection with multiple alert mechanisms

Customer Benefit

Regulatory compliance assurance, enhanced vehicle safety, and reliable operation in extreme conditions.

Our Process

Automotive-grade design with fail-safe architecture, secure firmware, and rigorous testing protocols.

Connectivity

Connectivity, Gateways & Diagnostics

Reliable vehicle networks

Our ECU model MEC-4G-IRNSS features 4G LTE, IRNSS, GPS, and Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity with multiple interface options. Key specifications include:

  • Model: MEC-4G-IRNSS
  • Dimensions: 120mm × 80mm × 35mm, Weight: 250g
  • Power: 9–36V DC, Current: ≤ 120mA @ 12V
  • LTE bands: B1/3/5/8 (FDD), B34/38/39/40/41 (TDD), GSM: B3/8
  • IP67 protection rating for all-weather operation
  • Multiple form factors: LCC, Mini PCIe-C, Mini PCIe
  • Comprehensive interfaces: (U)SIM, UART, Audio digital (PCM)
  • 2-year warranty with panel mount installation

Customer Benefit

Future-proof connectivity, compact form factor, and reliable performance across diverse vehicle platforms.

Our Process

Multi-band validation, environmental testing, and interface compatibility verification.
