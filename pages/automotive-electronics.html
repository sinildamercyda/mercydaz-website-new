MERCYDA MCD SPEED GOVERNOR

A Master Control Device (MCD) is a mechanical speed governor that limits the maximum speed of a vehicle without interacting with the vehicle’s ECU. It directly controls the throttle or fuel delivery mechanism, ensuring vehicles cannot exceed the preset speed limit, regardless of driver input.

Reliable Speed Control & Easy Integration

The Mercyda MCD Speed Governor is mechanically integrated into the vehicle’s throttle or fuel control system, ensuring precise speed limitation without relying on electronic systems or ECU connectivity. It delivers consistent and tamper-resistant performance, making it ideal for commercial vehicles, passenger transport, and fleet safety applications.
