Our Legacy Begins With Vision

Since 1994, our journey has been driven by innovation, determination, and a commitment to engineering excellence. The early products showcased in this archive represent the foundation of our technological evolution and the passion that continues to guide us today.

What started as pioneering communication and vehicle safety solutions has grown into a trusted legacy of reliability, quality, and forward-thinking design.

Legacy Archive

Foundational products from 1994 that pioneered our engineering excellence. No longer in production, but forever part of our story.

1994
Communication

MERCYDA Coin Pay Phones

Robust public payphone systems with integrated coin validation, call metering, and optional printer support. Designed for heavy-duty environments such as railways and bus stations, featuring battery backup, memory storage, and reliable operation during power interruptions.

  • CDMA / GSM Coin Pay Phone 1500-call memory, battery backup, optional printer
  • CDMA / GSM P.C.O Multi-coin support, programmable billing, LCD display
  • Alpha Numeric / Numeric P.C.O / Numeric P.C.O - 42v DTMF dialing, call timer, tone/pulse compatible
  • Wall Mounted Pay Phone 03D(W.M) Rugged enclosure, external battery support
  • Table Top Pay Phone (03D DLX / 03D ALX) Redial facility, EPABX compatible, compact design
Discontinued
2001
Billing Systems

MERCYDA 2001 STD PCO Monitors

Automated billing and call monitoring units built on low-power microchip technology with keyboard-programmable settings. Provided accurate call duration tracking, tariff calculation, and billing display with optional printing, making them ideal for commercial Public Call Office operations.

  • Citizen 555 Basic call metering, keyboard programmable
  • Epson 460 & Epson 42v LCD billing display, printer interface support
  • PCO-2001 Pro Programmable pulse rate, tariff settings, memory storage
  • PCO-2001 Enterprise Multi-line billing, advanced reporting capability
Discontinued
1994
Monitoring

MERCYDA Local Call Monitor & Conference Unit

Professional call monitoring and conferencing solutions with speaker output, paging functionality, and multi-line connectivity. Compatible with standard telephone exchanges and capable of operating on battery or mains power, supporting office communication control and coordination.

  • LCM-02D 8DIGITS 2-line monitoring, speaker output, paging support
  • LCM-07D 16DIGITS 4-line monitoring, display unit, recording output
  • Conference Facility Device-05D 4-party conference bridge, battery & mains operation
Discontinued
1994
Vehicle Systems

MERCYDA Prestige-811 Speed Governor

Early electronic speed limiting system for commercial vehicles, designed to improve road safety and regulatory compliance. Featured tamper-resistant controls, precise speed calibration, and reliable engine interfacing — forming the foundation for later MERCYDA speed management technologies.
