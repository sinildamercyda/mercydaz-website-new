MERCYDA Touch Sensing Panel Boards

MERCYDA Touch Sensing Panel Boards are advanced, reliable, and responsive control interfaces designed for modern industrial and consumer applications. Built with high-precision capacitive touch technology, these panels ensure seamless user interaction, durability, and efficient control across various environments.

MERCYDA Touch Sensing Panel Board Features

High-performance touch control with precision, durability, and smart integration

High Sensitivity Touch

Ultra-responsive capacitive touch sensing with high accuracy and minimal latency.

Multi-Touch Support

Supports multiple touch inputs for enhanced control and user interaction.

Durable Design

Designed for long-lasting performance with resistance to wear, moisture, and dust.

Low Power Consumption

Energy-efficient operation suitable for battery-powered and continuous-use systems.

Customizable Interface

Flexible design to support custom layouts, icons, and application-specific controls.

Wide Voltage Support

Compatible with various input voltages for industrial and embedded applications.

Noise Immunity

Reliable performance even in electrically noisy industrial environments.

Compact & Slim Design

Space-saving design ideal for modern control panels and embedded systems.

Smart Touch Control Solution

MERCYDA Touch Sensing Panel Boards are engineered to deliver intuitive and reliable control for a wide range of applications including home automation, industrial machinery, and smart devices. The panels provide smooth and accurate touch response, enhancing user experience and operational efficiency.

With robust design and advanced sensing technology, these panels ensure consistent performance even in challenging environments. They can be seamlessly integrated into existing systems, making them ideal for both new installations and upgrades.
