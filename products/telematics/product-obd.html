Mercyda OBD Tracker

The Mercyda OBD Tracker is a compact and intelligent vehicle tracking and diagnostics solution designed for fast deployment and ease of use. Plugged directly into the vehicle’s OBD-II port, it eliminates the need for wiring while providing instant access to location, engine data, and driving behaviour.

With integrated GNSS and cellular connectivity, the device delivers real-time tracking, vehicle health monitoring, fault code detection, and trip analytics. It is ideal for personal vehicles, taxis, rental cars, ride-hailing fleets, and light commercial vehicles where quick installation and portability are essential.

Core Capabilities

  • OBD-II plug-in design with instant activation
  • Live vehicle tracking and trip history
  • Engine diagnostics and fault code monitoring
  • Driving behaviour and usage analysis
  • Cloud-based platform with mobile & web access
  • Secure data transmission with encrypted communication
