Next-Generation AIS-140 Vehicle Tracking System

MERCYDA NEW VTS-140 A -4G LTE Vehicle Tracking System

The MERCYDA NEW VTS-140 A – 4G LTE is an advanced AIS-140 compliant vehicle tracking device designed for real-time tracking, fleet management, driver safety, and intelligent vehicle monitoring.

Explore Features View Specifications

Key Features

AIS-140 compliant vehicle tracking with real-time monitoring and intelligent alerts

Live Tracking

Real-time vehicle tracking using MercydaTrack Web, Android, and iOS applications with high-accuracy GPS and IRNSS positioning.

Route Playback

View complete route history and trip playback with detailed journey reports and maps..

Network Reliability

4G LTE connectivity with automatic 2G fallback ensures uninterrupted data transmission..

Live Alerts

Instant alerts for overspeed, ignition ON/OFF, power cut, tamper detection and geofence violations.

Multi-GNSS Support

Supports GPS, NavIC (IRNSS), GLONASS, Galileo and BDS for high-precision vehicle positioning and reliable navigation.

Speaker Support

Integrated speaker for emergency announcements, alerts and custom audio messages (optional).

Low Power Design

Optimized power usage with consumption below 200–300 mA using device outputs.

Rugged Design

IP67-rated enclosure designed for harsh automotive and environmental conditions.

Smart Vehicle Tracking Solution

The MERCYDA NEW VTS-140 A -4G LTE is built to deliver reliable, real-time vehicle visibility for fleet operators and transport providers. With accurate location tracking and advanced alerts, it helps improve safety, efficiency, and operational control.

Ideal for commercial fleets, logistics vehicles, public transport, and personal vehicles, the device ensures compliance while reducing operational costs.

Alerts & Safety Intelligence

  • Ignition ON / OFF Alerts
  • Main Battery Disconnect & Reconnect Alerts
  • Internal Battery Low & Recovery Alerts
  • Emergency (SOS) ON & OFF Alerts
  • GPS Enclosure Open Alert (Optional)
  • Over-the-Air Parameter Change Alerts
  • Harsh Braking, Harsh Acceleration & Rash Turning
  • Tilt Alert for accident detection
  • Geofence Entry & Exit Alerts
  • Offline Location History sync when GPRS is unavailable
