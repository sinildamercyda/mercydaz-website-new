VLTS + Panic Button Kit

The Mercyda VLTS + Panic Button Kit is a mandatory vehicle safety system as per AIS-140 guidelines issued by the Government of India. It is designed to provide immediate emergency response by enabling passengers or drivers to send SOS alerts with live vehicle location and audio communication.

Upon activation of the panic button, the system transmits vehicle identity, GPS / IRNSS location, and audio feed to the designated control room, enabling quick response from authorities. The solution is ideal for buses, taxis, school vehicles, public transport, and commercial fleets.

Kit Includes

  • AIS-140 compliant VLTS unit
  • Driver-side panic button
  • Passenger panic button (roof mounted)
  • Integrated microphone and speaker
  • Wiring harness and mounting accessories
