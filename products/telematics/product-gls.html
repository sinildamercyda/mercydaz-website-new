Rugged GLS-4G+ Tracking & Solution

The Mercyda GLS 4G Rugged is a heavy-duty, vehicle-grade telematics unit engineered for fleet, construction, mining and off-road applications. Built to withstand harsh environments, GLS 4G Rugged combines robust hardware, flexible I/O and advanced vehicle data collection (CAN/OBD) with reliable 4G connectivity and precision fuel monitoring. Designed for long service life and simple integration, it delivers vehicle location, diagnostics, driver behaviour and fuel analytics to your backend or third-party fleet management platforms.

Rugged Intelligence

  • 4G LTE connectivity (primary) with reliable cellular data transfer and OTA firmware updates.
  • CAN / OBD-II interface — read vehicle CAN bus and standard OBD PIDs; support for custom CAN frames and multi-protocol vehicles.
  • Advanced fuel monitoring — supports resistive (0–240 Ω), capacitive, level sender, and pulse flow sensors plus fuel theft/idle detection logic.
  • GNSS for location — high-sensitivity GPS/GLONASS receiver (explicitly not IRNSS).
  • Wide input voltage range — 9–90 V DC (suitable for 12 V, 24 V, and heavy-duty systems).
  • IP67 rated enclosure — dust tight and protected against temporary immersion.
  • Rugged industrial design — vibration and shock tolerant, extended temperature range.
  • Rich I/O — multiple digital/analog inputs, digital outputs, serial (RS232/RS485) and optional ignition sensing.
  • Local data buffer / SD card (optional) — stores data when network unavailable.
  • Security — TLS support for data transport, secure boot and signed firmware.
  • Flexible reporting — supports TCP/UDP/HTTP/MQTT, configurable intervals and event-based reporting.
  • Driver ID / RFID support — via external RFID reader (accessory).
  • Mounting & installation — multiple mounting options, tamper detection, and compact footprint.
