MERCYDA GLS-4G+ Advanced

An advanced 4G LTE GNSS tracking device with integrated audio, voice calling, and wide voltage support. Designed for real-time tracking, voice monitoring, and safety applications across light and heavy commercial vehicles.

MERCYDA GLS-4G+ Advanced Features

4G LTE tracking with voice communication and wide voltage support

Live Tracking

Real-time tracking with GPS and IRNSS support through MercydaTrack Web and mobile apps.

Voice Calling

Supports two-way voice calling between vehicle and control center for enhanced monitoring.

Audio Monitoring

Built-in microphone enables remote audio monitoring and in-vehicle voice communication.

Voice Recordings

Supports voice recording for safety, compliance, and operational review.

Wide Voltage Support

Operates on a wide voltage range from 9V to 90V DC, suitable for cars, buses, trucks and heavy equipment.

Advanced Alerts

Real-time alerts for ignition status, SOS, overspeed, geofence, power cut and tampering.

Advanced 4G LTE Tracking & Voice Solution

The MERCYDA GLS-4G+ Advanced combines vehicle tracking with voice communication to provide enhanced safety and operational visibility. Integrated audio features allow real-time interaction and monitoring.

Its wide voltage support makes it ideal for heavy-duty and commercial vehicles, while IP67 protection ensures durability in harsh environments.

Intelligence

  • Reliable data transmission with seamless network switching.
  • Accurate live tracking using GPS & IRNSS positioning.
  • Built-in microphone support for audio monitoring and communication.
  • Emergency (SOS) ON & OFF Alerts
  • Enables remote calling between control center and vehicle
  • Supports voice recording for monitoring, safety, and compliance use cases.
  • Compatible with cars, trucks, buses, and heavy-duty vehicles.
  • Alerts for overspeed, ignition, movement, power cut, and tampering.
  • Encrypted transmission for location and audio data.
  • IP67-rated enclosure for harsh environmental conditions.

GLS-4G+ Advanced Technical Specifications

Wide voltage, voice-enabled 4G LTE tracking solution

Parameter Specification
Model MERCYDA GLS-4G+ Advanced
Network Technology 4G LTE
LTE Bands LTE-FDD (B1 / B3 / B5 / B8), LTE-TDD (B34 / B38 / B39 / B40 / B41)
GSM Bands 900 / 1800 MHz
GNSS Support GPS, IRNSS
Tracking Accuracy ≤ 5 meters CEP
Audio Features Built-in microphone, two-way voice calling, voice recording

Protection Rating IP67
