SPEED GOVERNOR (UNIT)

Tamper-proof, non-invasive speed limiter for commercial fleets — AIS-034 certified, no engine modification required.

AIS-034 Certified

Smart, Non-Invasive Speed Compliance

Our standalone Speed Governor ensures fleet-wide adherence to legal speed limits — without altering OEM engine systems or voiding warranties.

Designed for quick retrofit installation, it interfaces safely with electronic throttle systems and includes self-diagnostics for maintenance confidence.

Non-Invasive No cutting, splicing, or engine modification
Self-Diagnostics Real-time LED status & error indication
Field-Adjustable Speed limit set by authorized technician

SPEED GOVERNOR (Standalone) — TECHNICAL SPECS

Core Function

  • Enforces pre-set max speed (configurable by technician)
  • Works with electronic throttle systems
  • No interference with engine ECU
  • Fail-safe: defaults to governed mode on fault

Installation & Diagnostics

  • Non-invasive wiring harness
  • LED status: Green (OK), Red (Fault)
  • Compatible with diagnostic tools
  • Sealed housing (IP65)

Category: Vehicle Safety

Certifications: AIS-034

Warranty: 24 months

Compatibility: Petrol & diesel engines with ETC (Electronic Throttle Control) — Tata, Ashok Leyland, Eicher, Mahindra, Force Motors
