MERCYDA VTS-140 A – 2G

AIS-140 Compliant Vehicle Tracking System

The MERCYDA VTS-140 A – 2G is a reliable, low-power, AIS-140 compliant Vehicle Tracking System designed for continuous vehicle monitoring, passenger safety, and regulatory compliance. Integrated seamlessly with the MercydaTrack platform, it ensures real-time tracking, intelligent alerts, and uninterrupted operations even in low-network conditions.

Alerts & Safety Intelligence

  • Ignition ON / OFF Alerts
  • Main Battery Disconnect & Reconnect Alerts
  • Internal Battery Low & Recovery Alerts
  • Emergency (SOS) ON & OFF Alerts
  • GPS Enclosure Open Alert (Optional)
  • Over-the-Air Parameter Change Alerts
  • Harsh Braking, Harsh Acceleration & Rash Turning
  • Tilt Alert for accident detection
  • Geofence Entry & Exit Alerts
  • Offline Location History sync when GPRS is unavailable
