Master Control Device Speed Governor

A Master Control Device (MCD) is a mechanical speed governor that limits the maximum speed of a vehicle without interacting with the vehicle’s ECU. It directly controls the throttle or fuel delivery mechanism, ensuring vehicles cannot exceed the preset speed limit, regardless of driver input.

Speed Governor Intelligence

  • Mechanical speed limiting with precision calibration.
  • Works independently of engine electronics.
  • Optional 4G data communication.
  • GNSS-based speed validation.
  • Event-based alerts (overspeed, tamper, power cut).
  • Secure firmware & encrypted data (for connected models).
  • Easy installation with minimal vehicle modification.
