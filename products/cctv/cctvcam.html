Mercyda 4G DOME CCTV Camera

The Mercyda 4G DOME Camera is a compact and rugged surveillance camera designed for remote and mobile security applications. With integrated 4G LTE connectivity, the camera functions independently of wired internet, making it ideal for vehicles, construction sites, warehouses, temporary installations, and infrastructure monitoring.

The camera delivers high-definition video streaming and recording over cellular networks, ensuring continuous visibility even in hard-to-reach locations. Its durable dome housing, stable power design, and wide operating temperature range ensure dependable performance in demanding environments.

Intelligent Surveillance Capabilities

  • Live video streaming over 4G LTE
  • Local microSD recording with remote playback
  • Infrared night vision with auto day/night switching
  • Two-way audio communication
  • Secure web and mobile app access
