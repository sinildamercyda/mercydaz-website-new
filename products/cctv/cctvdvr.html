Mercyda Dashboard Cam Monitor-DVR

Mercyda Dashboard Cam Monitor-DVR

The Mercyda Dashboard Cam Monitor-DVR is a compact and reliable in-vehicle surveillance system that combines a dashboard camera, DVR recording unit, and LCD monitor into a single device. It enables drivers and fleet operators to view live camera feeds and store high-quality video recordings for safety and documentation.

The system supports continuous and event-based recording with multi-camera input capability, making it ideal for buses, trucks, taxis, school vehicles, and commercial fleets. Its automotive-grade design ensures stable operation even in demanding road conditions.
