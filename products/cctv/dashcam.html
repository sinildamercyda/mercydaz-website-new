MERCYDA Dash Cameras – Key Features

Smart video recording with safety, security & fleet intelligence

Full HD Video Recording

High-resolution 1080p video ensures clear recording of road events and incidents.

Wide-Angle Lens

Wide field of view captures multiple lanes and surroundings for maximum coverage.

Loop Recording

Automatic overwrite of old footage ensures continuous recording without manual cleanup.

G-Sensor Event Lock

Automatically locks emergency footage during sudden braking, collision, or impact.

Night Vision Support

Mercyda Dash Camera

The Mercyda Dash Camera is a compact, high-performance in-vehicle video recording solution designed to improve driver safety and protect vehicle owners from disputes and false claims. It continuously records road footage while intelligently detecting critical events such as sudden braking or collisions.

Smart Dashcam Intelligence

  • Continuous front-facing video recording
  • Emergency event video locking via G-sensor
  • Time & date stamped footage
  • Optional GPS location tagging
  • Supports SD card & optional cloud storage
