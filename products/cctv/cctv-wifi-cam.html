Mercyda 4G Wi-Fi Camera

The Mercyda 4G Wi-Fi Camera is an advanced surveillance solution designed for locations where wired internet is unavailable or unreliable. By combining 4G LTE cellular connectivity with local Wi-Fi access, the camera allows quick installation, easy configuration, and continuous remote monitoring.

It supports Full HD video recording, infrared night vision, motion alerts, and secure remote access via mobile application and web interface. This makes it ideal for homes, offices, shops, construction sites, warehouses, farms, parking areas, and temporary installations.
