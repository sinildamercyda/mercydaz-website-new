ELECTRONIC CONTROL UNIT

The term ECU may refer to an Engine Control Unit, but it broadly denotes any Electronic Control Unit in automotive mechatronic systems — not just engine management.

AIS-140 Certified

Advanced Vehicle Intelligence

The Electronic Control Unit (ECU) is the brain of modern automotive mechatronic systems — managing everything from fuel injection and emissions to telematics integration.

While often associated with engine management, our ECU platform is a scalable, modular solution supporting multiple subsystems across diesel and petrol commercial vehicles.
